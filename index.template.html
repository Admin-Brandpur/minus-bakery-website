The Heart & Soul

Crafted by Minu in her tiny countryside kitchen.

It began with a single wooden spoon, a recipe book inherited from her grandmother, and a relentless curiosity for flour hydration. Today, Minu's Bakery is a sanctuary for those who appreciate the patience of wild sourdough yeast and the flawless layers of premium pasture-butter laminated pastry.

We never take shortcuts. No synthetic additives, no commercial volume-enhancers. Just local milk, freshly crushed herbs, single-origin cacao, and plenty of time.
